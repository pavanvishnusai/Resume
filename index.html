Choosing an optimal machine learning classifier for facial recognition:
Research intern under the guidance of Prof. Rohit Kumar Gupta

  • We researched all the classifiers and chose six to compare and submit the best based on accuracy and time consumed. To train and test data, we employed the ORL dataset, CV2, and a variety of additional libraries, followed by six classifications.
  • The best classifier is the LDA classifier, which has 97% accuracy and a time of 0.34 seconds.
